# XZ Backdoor Research

## Overview
The XZ backdoor refers to a malicious backdoor that was introduced to the Linux build of the xz utility within the liblzma library in versions 5.6.0 and later. This backdoor was discovered in early 2024 and raised significant concerns within the open-source community.
